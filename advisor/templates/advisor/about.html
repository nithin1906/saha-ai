Project Objective

The primary goal of SAHA-AI is to address the challenges faced by beginner and common investors. We aim to replace opaque, generic financial advice with a transparent, conversational, and deeply personalized experience. By combining machine learning with a user's portfolio context, SAHA-AI serves as an educational co-pilot, helping users understand the 'why' behind every recommendation.
